About Movie Recap App

What is Movie Recap App?

Movie Recap App is a powerful web-based tool that allows content creators, movie enthusiasts, and video editors to burn subtitles into videos with custom styling. Built with modern web technologies and powered by FFmpeg, it provides a seamless experience for creating professional recap videos.

How It Works

1

Upload

Upload your video file (MP4, MOV, AVI) and matching SRT subtitle file

2

Customize

Edit subtitle text, timings, and apply custom styling (font, size, color, background)

3

Render

FFmpeg burns subtitles directly into video frames for permanent styling

4

Download

Get your finished video ready for sharing on social media or platforms
